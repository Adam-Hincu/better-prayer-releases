# Better Prayer

A calm, beautifully simple desktop companion for your daily prayers and worship.
Better Prayer keeps the times you care about, gentle reminders, and a little daily
inspiration right on your desktop — so you can stay present without reaching for
your phone.

## Features

- **Accurate prayer times** — See the day's prayer schedule at a glance, tailored
  to your location.
- **Gentle reminders** — Optional notifications so you never miss a prayer, with
  controls to choose exactly which ones you want.
- **Device lockdown** — Lock down your device during prayer times so you can step
  away from the screen and focus fully on worship, free from distractions.
- **Fasting times** — Suhoor and Iftar times displayed clearly for your fasting
  days.
- **Daily verse** — A fresh verse each day to read and reflect on.
- **Quick access** — Jump straight to the Quran and daily supplications (Duas).
